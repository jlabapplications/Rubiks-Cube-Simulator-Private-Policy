Rubik's Cube Solver Privacy Policy

Our Rubik's Cube Solver application respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information that you may provide while using our app. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information Collection and Use:

  • We do not collect any personal data apart from the user's settings, which are stored locally on the user's device.
  • The user's settings may include preferences such as solving speed, color schemes, and solving methods.
  • We do not collect or store any identifiable information about our users.

2. Data Storage:

  • All user settings are stored locally on the user's device only.
  • We do not transmit or store any data externally, including on our servers or third-party servers.

3. Google AdMob:

  • Our app implements Google AdMob to display advertisements.
  • AdMob may collect anonymous device identifiers and usage data for targeted advertising purposes.
  • We do not have access to any personally identifiable information collected by AdMob.
  • Users can manage their AdMob settings through their device's settings or the AdMob opt-out page.

4. Data Security:

  • We take reasonable measures to secure any data stored locally on the user's device.
  • We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect user settings from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Children's Privacy:

  • Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.
  • If you believe that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate steps to remove such information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy:

  • We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time.
  • Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated Privacy Policy within the app.
  • Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@example.com.

By using our Rubik's Cube Solver app, you consent to the collection and use of your data as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
